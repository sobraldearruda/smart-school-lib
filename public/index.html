A School Library Management Platform for Librarians, Teachers, and Students

Overview

This project is a school library system that supports the management of users, books, authors, loans, and reading suggestions. The system accommodates three user roles: Librarians, Teachers, and Students, each with specific permissions and functionalities.

User Roles and Functionalities

All Users (Librarians, Teachers, Students)

  • Register, update, and delete their own profile
  • Access their own profile information and history

Librarians

  • Register, update, delete, and view any user‚Äôs profile and history
  • Manage books (register, update, delete, list, and view information)
  • Manage authors (register, update, delete, list, and view books by author)
  • Manage loans (register, update, delete, list, and view loan details)
  • Manage reading suggestions for any teacher user

Teachers

  • View books and authors (including their books)
  • Register their own book loans and view loan information
  • Manage their own reading suggestions (register, update, delete, view)

Students

  • View books and authors (including their books)
  • Register their own book loans and view loan information
